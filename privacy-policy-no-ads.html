Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 22, 2024

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Recipe Parser ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for our mobile application ("App").

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

2.1 No Advertising

This App does not display advertisements and does not collect advertising IDs or any advertising-related data.

2.2 No Analytics

We do not use analytics services or track your app usage. We have no way of knowing how you use the App.

2.3 No User Accounts

We do not require user accounts or registration. We do not collect email addresses, names, or any personal identifiers.

3. Local Data Storage

All data created in the App is stored locally on your device only:

  • Recipes you save or create
  • Shopping lists
  • Inventory items
  • Meal plans
  • Favorites and preferences
  • App settings (measurement system, etc.)

This data is never transmitted to our servers, shared with third parties, or accessible to us. It remains on your device and is only accessible to you. If you uninstall the App, this data will be deleted.

4. Recipe URLs

When you parse a recipe from a URL, we fetch the recipe data from that website to display it in the App. We do not:

  • Store the URLs you visit
  • Track which websites you access
  • Share URLs with third parties
  • Collect any information from the websites you visit

5. Optional Features

5.1 Barcode Scanning

If you use the barcode scanning feature:

  • Scans are processed locally on your device
  • Product information may be fetched from Open Food Facts (a public database)
  • We do not store or track your scans

5.2 Receipt Scanning

If you use the receipt scanning feature:

  • Images are processed locally on your device using on-device text recognition
  • Images are not stored or transmitted
  • We have no access to your receipt images

5.3 Camera/Photo Access

The App may request camera or photo access only for barcode and receipt scanning features. Images are processed locally and never stored or shared.

6. In-App Purchases

Premium features are purchased through Google Play's in-app purchase system. We do not:

  • Have access to your payment information (handled securely by Google)
  • Store your purchase history
  • Share purchase data with third parties

Purchase status is stored locally on your device only to enable/disable premium features.

7. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: For barcode and receipt scanning (optional feature)
  • Storage/Photos: For selecting images for receipt scanning (optional feature)
  • Internet: For fetching recipe data from URLs (only when you request it)

All permissions are optional and only used for the specific features you choose to use. We do not use permissions to collect data.

8. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with anyone. We do not sell, rent, or trade any information because we don't collect any information to share.

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to access, modify, or delete. All your app data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

10. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any data, there are no privacy concerns for children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
