## Tips for Play Store

1. **Update regularly** - Keep descriptions fresh as you add features
2. **Use keywords** - Include terms like "recipe", "cooking", "meal planning", "shopping list"
3. **Highlight benefits** - Focus on what users can do, not just features
4. **Call to action** - End with "Download now" or similar
5. **Emojis** - Use sparingly (I've included some, but you can remove if preferred)
